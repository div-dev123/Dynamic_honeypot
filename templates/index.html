What is a Honeypot?

A honeypot is a security mechanism that creates a virtual trap to lure cyber attackers. It is a decoy system set up to attract and detect unauthorized access attempts. Honeypots help in understanding attack patterns and techniques, thus enhancing cybersecurity defenses.

Importance of a Honeypot

Honeypots play a crucial role in cybersecurity by providing valuable insights into the tactics, techniques, and procedures (TTPs) used by attackers. They help in identifying vulnerabilities, improving threat detection, and developing more robust security measures.
